Aisyah Bot Discord - Music Player (2023-2024)

Aisyah bot is a Discord bot designed to play music automatically on Discord servers. Developed between 2023 and 2024, this bot comes with various features like /play, /autoplay, /join, and /leave, allowing users to easily control music playback.
